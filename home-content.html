Animation Internship, June - December 2022

Who is AOF?

Accion Opportunity Fund is a non-profit lender that offers low interest loans to small businesses. Learn more here.

As an animator on the marketing team I did a combination of short motion graphics and longer 1-2 minute animated videos based on stories from their clients.


My Biggest Takeaways:

  • Team structure - feedback from multiple sources, one focused on the creative side, the other on logistics and marketing
  • Juggling projects - working on long and short projects simultaneously, keeping track of multiple deadlines using Asana
  • Corporate identity - colors, fonts, grids, templates - working with graphic design intern to adhere to strict guidelines

About the Video

  • "La Geurrera's Kitchen" is a family-owned restaurant in California who recieved services from AOF. This video was created to highlight an immigrant-owned small business during Hispanic Heritage month.
  • This video was created entirely by me - from sound editing to storyboarding to final cuts. More on my video process here.
  • It was very rewarding to create a video celebrating Mexican culture, especially because one of my bosses was from Mexico and was very attached to the project.
