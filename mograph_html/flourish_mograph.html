Visual Design Internship

Meet Sunnie

"Flourish" is a personal well-being app currently in development.

Many of my projects involved collaborating with team members to bring illustrations of "Sunnie" - the app's cheerful sun mascot - to life through animation.

My multi-program workflow started with taking illustrations from Figma into Adobe Illustrator, divide them into working layers, and then animating them in Adobe After Effects.

Finally, I would export the animations into a modern web-based animation format called "Lottie," which is vector scalable and more efficiant than other image types.
