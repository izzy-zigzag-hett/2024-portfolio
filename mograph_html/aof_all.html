Who is AOF?

Accion Opportunity Fund is a non-profit lender that offers low interest loans to small businesses. Learn more here.

Video bumper, created for AOF


What was my role?

At this remote internship, I worked closely with 3 others on the marketing team - a graphic design lead and intern, and a creative director. Erin, our lovely creative director, worked closely with me on variety of motion-based projects, such as:

  • Templates - adding subtle movement to existing graphic design for Instagram, Facebook, and Vimeo
  • Animated videos - 1-2 minutes, based on stories from AOF's clients
  • Editing video and audio - condensing and organizing pre-recorded footage
  • Social media - gifs, "reels," and web content

Social media post templates:

The goal with these animations was to create enough subtle movement to catch an onlooker's eye, but not distract from the experience of reading the content. These animations took iterative feedback from the team's creative director in order to find that equilibrium.

My Biggest Takeaways:

  • Marketing matters! - This work felt rewarding, as I knew our marketing content was educating people on the grants and opportunities AOF offered
  • Team structure - I recieved feedback from multiple sources, one focused on the creative side, the other on logistics and marketing
  • Juggling projects - I worked on long and short projects simultaneously, keeping track of multiple deadlines using Asana
  • Corporate identity - Colors, fonts, grids, templates - I worked closely with the graphic design intern to adhere to consistent guidelines
