Animation with purpose

Accion Opportunity Fund (AOF) is a non-profit lender that gives low interest loans to small businesses.

As an animator on the marketing team I did a combination of short motion graphics and longer 1-2 minute animated videos based on stories from their clients.

This video was created start to finish for Hispanic Heritage month. The audio is taken from a video interview with Reyna, a restaurant owner who recieved resources from AOF.

The animated video process:

Video projects like this one were great practice for understanding the production process from start to finish.

For projects at this scale, this was my step-by-step process:

  1. Brainstorm concise story
  2. Edit audio to appropriate length
  3. Storyboard visuals for each part of the audio
  4. "Rough out" animation - creating and placing illustrations for each scene
  5. Animate each scene, getting feedback piece by piece
  6. Animation transitions, typography, final touches

This video was based on an interview with Jackson, a 17-year-old apparel brand owner. I was a part of the interview process, brainstorming and asking him questions about his business and experiences with AOF. This video was never finished due to complications with the interviewee.
